Philadelphia Real Estate Law, Expertly Navigated

Real estate matters in the Philadelphia metropolitan area involve a unique intersection of state law, local ordinances, municipal tax codes, and administrative processes. Christopher W. Dean brings firsthand knowledge of this landscape — including his prior experience representing the City of Philadelphia — to every client engagement.

Whether you are acquiring commercial property, resolving a title dispute, challenging an inflated tax assessment, or navigating a landlord-tenant conflict, Dean Law provides the rigorous, detail-oriented counsel your transaction or dispute demands.

Services We Provide

Property Acquisitions & Sales

Thorough due diligence, contract review, title examination, and closing representation for residential and commercial property transactions of all complexity levels.

Title Disputes & Clearance

Resolution of title defects, encumbrances, boundary disputes, and adverse claims that cloud property ownership and impede transactions.

Zoning & Land Use

Representation before zoning boards, administrative agencies, and courts on matters of permitted use, variances, special exceptions, and development approvals.

Tax Assessment Appeals

Strategic appeals of inflated property tax assessments, leveraging intimate knowledge of the City of Philadelphia's assessment process to achieve meaningful reductions.

Landlord–Tenant Matters

Representation for landlords and tenants in lease disputes, eviction proceedings, habitability issues, and security deposit conflicts under Pennsylvania law.

Real Estate Litigation

Forceful advocacy in property disputes before Philadelphia-area courts, including breach of contract, specific performance, and quiet title actions.

Real estate is often among the most significant investments individuals and businesses make. At Dean Law, we treat each transaction and dispute with the gravity it deserves — providing careful analysis, clear communication, and decisive action.
