Navigating Government With Government-Side Knowledge

Municipal law is a specialized and often underappreciated field. When individuals, businesses, or organizations encounter the apparatus of government — whether through code enforcement actions, tax assessments, public records disputes, or regulatory proceedings — they face an opponent with institutional resources, established procedures, and significant leverage.

Christopher W. Dean has sat on that side of the table. Having served as legal counsel for the City of Philadelphia, he understands precisely how municipal agencies operate, how they make decisions, and where their arguments have vulnerabilities. This insider knowledge is not hypothetical — it has directly shaped successful outcomes for Dean Law clients dealing with the City and other governmental entities.

Whether you are challenging a government action, seeking compliance with a regulatory requirement, or pursuing a public records request, Dean Law brings the credibility, knowledge, and strategy that this specialized area demands.

Municipal Law Services

Government Entity Representation

Counsel for municipalities, agencies, authorities, and boards in governance matters, inter-governmental disputes, and regulatory proceedings.

Regulatory Compliance

Guidance and representation to help individuals and organizations achieve and maintain compliance with local, state, and municipal regulatory frameworks.

Municipal Tax Litigation

Aggressive representation in disputes with taxing authorities over real estate, business privilege, and other municipal taxes in Philadelphia and surrounding jurisdictions.

Code Enforcement Matters

Defense and resolution of code enforcement violations, licensing disputes, and administrative penalties issued by City of Philadelphia agencies.

Public Records (RTKL)

Representation in Right-to-Know Law proceedings — both in pursuing access to public records and defending against overbroad public disclosure requests.

Administrative Hearings

Representation before Philadelphia administrative boards, the Board of Revision of Taxes, Zoning Board of Adjustment, and related administrative forums.

When government stands across from you — with its resources, procedures, and institutional inertia — you need counsel who knows how to navigate that terrain with precision. Dean Law provides exactly that.
