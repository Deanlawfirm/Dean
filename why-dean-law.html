The Dean Law
Distinction

When you retain Dean Law, you retain Christopher W. Dean — a seasoned attorney who has argued before courts, represented municipal governments, and prosecuted cases at the county level. That breadth of experience is not theoretical. It shapes how every matter is analyzed, every strategy is developed, and every client is served.

We are selective about the matters we accept. This is by design. It ensures that every client receives meaningful time, thoughtful preparation, and direct access to senior legal counsel throughout their engagement.

Direct Partner Access

Your case is handled personally by Mr. Dean from inception through resolution — not delegated to junior associates or paralegal staff.

Government-Side Insight

Having represented municipal entities and served as a prosecutor, we understand how the other side thinks — and how to use that knowledge to your advantage.

Results-Oriented Strategy

We develop litigation and transactional strategies tailored to your goals, focused on achieving measurable, practical outcomes — not billable hours.

Deep Philadelphia Roots

Born from the city's legal community, we have strong relationships with the courts, agencies, and institutions that matter to your case.
