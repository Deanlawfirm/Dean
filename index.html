The Attorney

Christopher W.
Dean, Esq.

Founder & Principal Attorney

Christopher W. Dean is the Founder and Principal Attorney of Dean Law. Educated at the California School of Law, Mr. Dean has built a distinguished legal career spanning real estate, civil litigation, and municipal governance in the greater Philadelphia region.

Mr. Dean previously served as an Assistant District Attorney in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, and has served as counsel representing the City of Philadelphia in complex real estate and tax litigation matters.
